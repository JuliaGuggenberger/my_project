Phase II - Variation in costs

Compared to the previous phase, the costs of each travel mode may change. This is meant to reflect real-world conditions. For example, a bike ride might become more costly (or inconvenient) if it's raining, or if you need to carry something bulky, like a cake. On the other hand, biking could become more attractive if you want to exercise, while rising fuel prices or road closures might make driving more expensive.
