Your payoff is the amount of money you can take home after the experiment. It depends on how much of your weekly budget remains at the end of these six weeks. This leftover budget will be adjusted to fall within a range of 200 to 400. In addition, one participant will have the chance to receive up to 700. The final amount is always rounded to the nearest 5.
